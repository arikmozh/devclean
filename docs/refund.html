Refund Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026 · devclean Pro by DevForge (arikmozh)

14-day money-back guarantee

If devclean Pro isn't working out for you, no worries. You can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase, no questions asked. We'd rather you be happy than stuck with something you don't use.

How to request a refund

Just reach out through either of these:

  • Open an issue on GitHub with your order details
  • Email us via your LemonSqueezy receipt (hit reply on the purchase confirmation email)

Include your license key or the email you used to purchase so we can find your order quickly.

Processing time

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The actual time it takes to appear on your statement depends on your bank or payment provider.

What happens after a refund

Once your refund is processed, your Pro license key will be deactivated. Pro features will no longer be available. Your subscription will be cancelled automatically.

Free tier is always available

Regardless of refund status, the free version of devclean remains fully available. You can continue scanning and cleaning your disk with all free features — no restrictions.
