1. What you're getting

devclean is an all-in-one disk cleanup CLI for developers. It scans and cleans node_modules, Docker, Homebrew, pip, Cargo, Xcode, and package manager caches. It's available as a free CLI with optional Pro features unlocked via a paid license key.

2. License

Your Pro license is personal and grants access to one user. You may install it on multiple machines you own, but sharing your license key with others is not permitted. The free tier is available to everyone without a license.

3. Pro license keys

After purchasing, you'll receive a license key. Activate it by running devclean activate <key>. Your key is validated locally and may periodically check for validity. Keep your key private.

4. Billing

Pro subscriptions are billed monthly at $6/mo through LemonSqueezy, our payment processor. You can cancel anytime from your LemonSqueezy customer portal. Cancellation stops future charges, and your Pro access remains active until the end of the billing period.

5. No warranty

devclean is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. We do our best to keep it working well, but we can't guarantee it will be error-free or uninterrupted. Disk cleanup operations are performed at your own discretion — always use --dry-run first if you're unsure.

6. Privacy & data

devclean runs entirely on your machine. We don't collect analytics, telemetry, or usage data. No file paths, disk contents, or system information ever leaves your computer. The only network request is an occasional license validation check for Pro users.
