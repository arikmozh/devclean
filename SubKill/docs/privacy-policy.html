SubKill Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

TL;DR: SubKill does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

Our Philosophy

SubKill is built on a simple principle: your financial data is yours alone. We created a subscription tracker that doesn't track you. No accounts, no servers, no analytics, no ads.

Data Collection

SubKill collects zero personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • We do not collect usage analytics or behavioral data
  • We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not use tracking pixels or fingerprinting

Data Storage

All subscription data you enter into SubKill is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Widget data is shared between the main app and the widget extension using Apple's App Groups, which is a secure on-device communication channel — no network involved.

Local Notifications

SubKill may send you local notifications to remind you about upcoming subscription renewals, but only if you grant permission. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external push notification service.

In-App Purchases

SubKill offers optional tips through Apple's StoreKit. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive any personal or payment information from these transactions.

Data Sharing

We share your data with no one. Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to share, sell, or transfer to third parties.

Data Export

You can export your subscription data as a CSV file at any time from the Settings screen. This export is generated locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

SubKill does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Given our commitment to zero data collection, we don't anticipate significant changes.
