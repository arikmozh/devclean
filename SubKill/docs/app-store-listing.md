```
You're paying for subscriptions you forgot about. The average person wastes $133 every month on services they don't use. SubKill shows you exactly where your money goes — and helps you take it back.

WATCH YOUR MONEY DRAIN

SubKill's unique Water Tank visualization shows your subscription spending dripping away in real-time. The more you spend, the faster it drains. It's a wake-up call you can't ignore.

SMART INSIGHTS

SubKill doesn't just list your subscriptions — it analyzes them:

• Identifies your biggest money drain
• Spots duplicate spending across categories
• Flags streaming service overload
• Suggests switching to annual billing (save 15-20%)
• Warns you before renewals hit

EVERYTHING YOU NEED

• Quick Add — 40+ popular services pre-loaded (Netflix, Spotify, ChatGPT, and more)
• Smart Categories — Entertainment, Productivity, AI Tools, Health, and 12 more
• Multiple Currencies — USD, EUR, GBP, ILS support
• Renewal Reminders — Never get surprised by a charge again
• Beautiful Statistics — Charts, spending breakdowns, fun cost comparisons
• Home Screen Widgets — Track spending at a glance (Small, Medium, Large)
• CSV Export — Your data, your way
• Share Stats — Show friends how much you've saved

THE SATISFACTION OF CANCELLING

When you cancel a subscription in SubKill, it's not just a checkmark. It's a celebration — complete with smash animations, confetti, and haptic feedback that makes saving money feel incredible.

WHAT MAKES SUBKILL DIFFERENT

No subscription. No ads. No tracking. No account required.

While every other subscription tracker charges you a monthly fee to track your monthly fees, SubKill is a one-time purchase. We practice what we preach.

Your data stays on your device. We don't collect analytics, don't serve ads, and don't sell your information. Check our privacy policy — it's refreshingly short.

PERFECT FOR

• Budget-conscious spenders who want visibility
• People with 5+ subscriptions they've lost track of

Track it. See it. Kill it. Take back your money with SubKill.
```
